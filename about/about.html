About The Mission: Hot Chocolate

Hi! My name is Thalia Lambros, and I am a lover of a hot chocolate. In this blog, I will be tasting my way around the Mission district’s hot chocolate offerings in a series of three locations. As a hot chocolate enthusiast living in San Francisco, I’m on a never-ending quest to discover the city's most indulgent and delicious cocoa creations. Whether it’s a velvety, rich classic or a unique gourmet twist, there’s nothing I’d rather do than curl up with a perfectly crafted cup of hot cocoa to warm both the body and soul. Being from Missoula Montana, there are no gourmet cafes to pick up a mug of cinnamon cocoa or traditional Oaxacan hot choco, which is why I am so excited to take advantage of all the chocolatey goodness SF has to offer. San Francisco, with its diverse food scene and passion for artisanal flavors, is the perfect place for a hot chocolate lover like me to explore. Beginning with this short series of cafes in the Mission district, I’m excited to share my journey of finding the best cocoa spots this part of the city has to offer—one delicious sip at a time.
