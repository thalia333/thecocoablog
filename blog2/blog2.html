About Dandelion Chocolate Four Barrell Café de Olla

Place 2 Four Barrell 375 Valencia St, San Francisco, CA 94110

Four Barrell Coffee, just down the street from Dandelion Chocolate, is unassuming from the front. There’s no sign or name out front alerting you of a cute coffee shop, just tables outside full of people (if it’s a sunny day) and the smell of freshly ground coffee. The interior is a big open space with a sleek hipster vibe, all wood and warm colors, with small tables and local art on the walls. It is the perfect place to work on homework or an individual project or catch up with a friend. Or, to get hot chocolate. The line was not long, and I spent $6 on hot chocolate. They have one option, which is their regular hot chocolate made with chocolate ganache. It does contain dairy, so beware of that if you are lactose intolerant. They served my drink in a plain white mug, which is good for sustainability but didn’t add much in terms of brand identity. There was quite a bit of foam on the top, but once I made it past that, the texture was creamy and not too thick, and the taste was a beautiful balance of sweetness and bitterness. If you’re a dark chocolate lover like me, you will appreciate this hot chocolate! I also recommend getting a spoon to stir it with because since the chocolate is heavier than the milk, it tends to sink to the bottom of the cup.
